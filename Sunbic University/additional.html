Vision and Mission!


Vision


A World-class university, fostering and empowering the society in building and serving the nation.


Mission


The mission of Sunbic University is to build the nation and to contribute in global community development by providing world class education by means of :
Educating Sunbician to develop exemplary characters through holistic approach that meets global standards. Educating Sunbician to be leaders with multiple skills by providing holistic approach using global standards and hands-on entrepreneurial learning experiences.

Resolving the nation’s issues through high impact research. Guiding Sunbician to resolve the nation’s issues through high impact and internationally recognized research by integrating the nusantara’s diversity and wisdom for enriching quality education.

Fostering Sunbician as lifelong learners through self-enrichment. Nourishing Sunbician spirit to develop exemplary character through personal and professional growth by leveraging best practices for lifelong learning.

Empowering Sunbician to continuously improve society’s quality of life. Transforming Sunbician to be empowering agents through community service programs that improve the quality of life needed for building and serving the nation.

Being the main driver to enrich the Sunbic Higher Education system. Enriching the quality of Higher Education by utilizing the best practices to consolidate a world class Education system with a unified spirit to foster and empower.
